What is an indie head massage? Indie Head Massage, also known as “Champissage”, is a form of massage therapy based on the ancient healing system of “Ayurveda”. Warm oil is applied to the scalp and hair, and combined with deep therapeutic massage to the face, scalp, neck and shoulders.

What is cupping? Cupping therapy is an ancient form of alternative medicine in which a therapist puts special cups on your skin for a few minutes to create suction. People get it for many purposes, including to help with pain, inflammation, blood flow, relaxation and well-being, and as a type of deep-tissue massage.

Read More...

What is Swedish massage? Swedish massage is one of the most commonly offered massage techniques. It’s sometimes called a classic massage. The technique aims to promote relaxation by releasing muscle tension. Swedish massage is gentler than deep tissue massage and better suited for people interested in relaxation and tension relief.
